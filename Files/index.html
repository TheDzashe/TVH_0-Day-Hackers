About Kaci Connect

Empowering South African township businesses with innovative financial solutions.

Our Mission

Kaci Connect was founded to address the unique financial challenges faced by township entrepreneurs. We believe every business owner deserves access to simple, effective financial tools that help them grow and thrive.

What We Do

We provide an intuitive platform that integrates with popular South African payment systems like YOCO, MTN, and Zapper, making it easy to track sales, manage expenses, and stay compliant with SARS regulations.

Our Team

Our diverse team of financial experts, developers, and business advisors are passionate about supporting the growth of township economies across South Africa.
